Paneer Butter Tika Masala

Paneer Butter Tika Masala is a rich and creamy Indian dish made with paneer (Indian cottage cheese) cooked in a spiced tomato and butter sauce.

Ingredients

  • 200g paneer, cubed
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tomatoes, pureed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Heat butter in a pan and sauté the chopped onions until golden brown.
  2. Add the ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute.
  3. Add the pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, and garam masala. Cook until the oil separates from the masala.
  4. Add the cubed paneer and mix well to coat the paneer with the masala.
  5. Add the heavy cream and simmer for 5 minutes. Adjust salt to taste.
