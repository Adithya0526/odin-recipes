Pani Puri

Pani Puri is a popular Indian street food made of crispy puris filled with spicy water and various toppings.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup urad dal (split black gram)
  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil for frying
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 2 potatoes, boiled and mashed
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 2 tablespoons oil

Steps

  1. Soak the urad dal and rice separately for 30 minutes.
  2. Grind the soaked urad dal and rice into a smooth batter.
  3. Add salt and turmeric powder to the batter and let it ferment for 8-10 hours.
  4. In a pan, add oil and cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add the chopped onions and sauté until golden brown.
  5. Add the ginger-garlic paste and red chili powder. Cook for a minute.
  6. Add the mashed potatoes and mix well. Cook for 5 minutes.
