Classic Waffles

Classic Waffles are crispy on the outside, soft on the inside, and perfect for breakfast. They are best served warm with syrup, fruits, or butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 3/4 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Mix the dry ingredients in a bowl.
  3. In another bowl, whisk the wet ingredients.
  4. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, stirring until just mixed.
  5. Heat the waffle iron and spray with cooking spray.
  6. Pour the batter onto the waffle iron and cook according to the manufacturer's instructions.
